Our Founder

Tommy Diz Fab opened Tommys Pizzeria almost a decade ago. Tommy has always been eager to open and operate a Pizzeria. Tommys grandfather once owned a pizzaria at the location that Tommys Pizzeria sits at today but had to give up his dream when WWII began. Now Tommy relieves his grandfathers dream and passion with the great tasting Tommys Pizzeria

Food Quality

When it comes to Pizzas Pastas and everything else Tommys Pizzeria nothing but the best of quality food. The chefs and Tommy make sure it a priority to only serve the best their is to offer. Our head chef selectively goes out each morning and picks the freshest of ingredients from the local markets. All food is made when ordered with passion and confidence.
